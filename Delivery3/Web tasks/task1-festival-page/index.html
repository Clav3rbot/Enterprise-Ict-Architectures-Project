About The Festival

Experience the ultimate summer celebration where world-class music, art, and beach vibes converge for an unforgettable three-day journey.

SunWave Festival brings together over 100 international artists across 5 stages, featuring everything from electronic dance music to indie rock, hip-hop, and live acoustic performances. Set against the stunning backdrop of Miami Beach, our festival offers more than just music.
